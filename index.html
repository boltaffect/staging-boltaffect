STEM Camp Management

CampInfinity is a unique STEM camp that runs educational camp programs for gifted kids. Because the staff wants to ensure a good fit between their camp program and the campers that attend they have an admissions process similar to a private school. But managing admissions is tricky business, so CampInfinity contacted us to help them meet the challenge.

  • Wed, 01 Oct 2014
  • apps, marketing
  • CampInfinity
  • campinfinity.com

Automation

We built a camper application and registration system for CampInfinity to reduce the labor in accepting campers. The system we built allows for the Ci staff to easily manage applications, registrations, camper information, payments, and camper checklists.

HIPAA Security

We’re also pleased to say that we provided CampInfinity a HIPAA compliant system, since camper medical information is collected. Additionally, our HIPAA solution was inexpensive, which made a secure system feasible.

Integrated

The management app acts as a central portal for parents to login and get information about camp. But we also provided seamless integration with CampInfinity’s marketing site, which eliminates double entry and makes it easy to have accurate information for parents (no matter where they’re looking).

Job Applicant Filtering App

TalentFlood is a startup that came to us with spreadsheets and big ideas to change how businesses find talent. We built a sophisticated app for TalentFlood that allows job hunters to find the perfect job, and businesses the ability to find perfect employees.

  • Wed, 01 Oct 2014
  • apps
  • TalentFlood
  • talentflood.com

Allow for Different User Types

This project involved creating a system that allows three different user types the ability to create accounts and manage their profiles: Talent, Hiring Managers, and Organization Employees.

Multi-step Forms

The Talent create a profile that includes all of their resume information, life experiences, and an advanced personality test. All of these steps are displayed on their own screens and saved individuality for both ease of use for the Talent and data loss protection. We track which sections are complete and about how much more time an applicant will need to complete the forms.

Manage Your Organization

The Hiring Manager users create a company account and then invite their employees to join the system. Hiring Managers are administrators, but they can select additional users to also have admin privileges.

Automation Made Easy

The Hiring Managers create job listings with complex filtering that is made simple through the easy to use setup screen. We wrote a system that listens for any Job Listing that is added or updated and then automatically applies a fancy matching routine to rank Talent against the Job Listing. This allows the Hiring Manager to see a short list of candidates to interview.
